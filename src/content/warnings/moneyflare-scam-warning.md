## Quick summary

We have downgraded **MoneyFlare** from a recommended platform to a **scam-watch warning**. The evidence — Trustpilot complaints, a low independent scam-checker score, a "guaranteed AI returns" model, and our own contact with support — points to serious problems getting money **out** of the platform. **Do not deposit until these issues are resolved.**

## Why we flagged it

- **Withdrawals blocked or delayed.** Trustpilot reviews cluster around users unable to cash out larger balances.
- **Deposit-to-withdraw demands.** When we contacted MoneyFlare's support, we were told there is significant red tape around withdrawals, and users can be **required to make additional deposits before a withdrawal is processed.** No legitimate platform asks you to deposit more to release your own funds.
- **High, guaranteed-sounding "AI bot" returns** — a pattern regulators repeatedly warn about.
- **Not FCA-registered** for UK users.

## What you should do

- **Try to withdraw now** — and **never deposit more to "unlock" a withdrawal.**
- Document everything (screenshots, transaction IDs, support chats).
- Report to **Action Fraud** (UK) and check the **FCA** crypto-scam guidance.
- Ignore any "fund recovery" service that contacts you — that's a common follow-up scam.
