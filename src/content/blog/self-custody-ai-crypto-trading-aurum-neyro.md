## What is AURUM Neyro?

**Neyro is AURUM's AI-powered trading product** — a network of smart agents that execute trades through smart contracts while keeping **full control of funds in your own wallet**. You never deposit capital with AURUM. You never hand over a private key. The AI signals route through audited contracts on Polygon, and your USDT stays in a wallet that only *you* can sign from.

This is the difference between **custodial copy-trading** (where the platform holds your money and could vanish overnight) and **non-custodial AI trading** (where the bot has signing permission for one specific contract action and nothing else). For a deeper primer on the model, read our companion article: [Self-Custody AI Crypto Trading Explained](/blog/self-custody-ai-crypto-trading-aurum-neyro).

## How does Neyro actually work?

Neyro runs on three layers that never touch each other directly:

1. **The AI layer.** Trading agents analyse market data, follow strategy settings, and produce trade signals.
2. **The smart-contract layer.** Signals are executed on-chain through a Polygon smart contract you've explicitly approved.
3. **Your wallet.** Funds remain in your custody. The contract has scoped permission to open and close positions on a decentralised exchange — and nothing else.

If AURUM's servers go offline tomorrow, your funds are still in your wallet. That's the entire point. For a side-by-side comparison with custodial copy-trading bots, see our [Copy AI Trading guide](/blog/copy-ai-trading-best-platforms-2026).

## Compute Balance, Burn Rate and "Funded" — what those numbers mean

When you first land on the Neyro stats page, you'll see metrics that look unfamiliar. Here's what they actually are:

- **Compute Balance** — resources used to *run* the trading agent. This is **not your deposit** and does not affect your funds.
- **Balance Consumed** — how much of those compute resources has been used.
- **Burn Rate** — how quickly compute is being consumed.
- **Estimated Duration** — how long the compute resources may last at the current burn rate.
- **Funded** — refers only to the **amount allocated to the strategy**, not your full wallet balance.

If some values appear before any trading activity, that's because they're system-initialised. It does *not* mean funds are being spent.
