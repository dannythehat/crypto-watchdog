## How to Vet a cryptobit.live Alternative Before You Deposit

A round-up only helps if you can apply it. Here's the short version of how we'd check any platform on this list, or any other, before moving funds.

- **Find out who runs it.** A named, contactable team beats an anonymous one. If you can't tell who's behind a site, that's information in itself.
- **Test a withdrawal early.** Deposit a small amount, then take it back out before you commit more. A platform that makes withdrawals slow or conditional is telling you something.
- **Check the regulatory picture.** In the UK, you can search the [FCA Register](https://register.fca.org.uk/) to see whether a firm is authorised, and the FCA's [warning list](https://www.fca.org.uk/scamsmart/warning-list-unauthorised-firms) flags firms operating without permission.
- **Treat guaranteed returns as a red flag.** No legitimate platform can promise a fixed daily profit. The moment you see one, stop.
- **Read independent results, not just the homepage.** Marketing tells you what a platform wants you to think. Documented testing tells you what actually happens.

None of this takes long, and it's a lot cheaper than learning the hard way.
