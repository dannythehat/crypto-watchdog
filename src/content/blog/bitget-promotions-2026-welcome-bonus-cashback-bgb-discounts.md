## The Bitget Welcome Bundle

The headline offer for new accounts is the **Welcome Bundle**, currently advertised at **up to 6,200 USDT** in combined rewards. Bitget structures it as a series of small task-based vouchers rather than one big lump sum.

The bundle typically includes:

## BGB Holder Discounts

BGB is Bitget's platform token. Holding it unlocks a permanent fee discount that stacks with promotional vouchers.

Standard discount tiers:

- **Tier 1 (small holdings):** ~20% off spot fees.
- **Tier 2 (mid-size holdings):** ~30% off spot, partial futures discount.
- **VIP tiers:** up to 50% off futures fees plus dedicated account manager.

The discount applies automatically once you hold BGB in your spot account. You do **not** need to lock or stake it to unlock the basic 20% off — though staking via Bitget Earn does add a small APY.

A word of caution: BGB price is exchange-correlated. If Bitget's volume drops, BGB usually drops with it. Treat BGB as a utility token for fee savings, not a long-term investment.

## Launchpad and Launchpool Farms

Bitget runs both a **Launchpad** (commit BGB or USDT for a token sale allocation) and a **Launchpool** (stake assets to farm a new token for free).

Launchpool campaigns are usually the better risk-adjusted play because you keep your principal — only the farmed token carries price risk. APYs in the launch token can briefly exceed 100%, but they decay quickly as more participants join.
