The original cryptocurrency and the most decentralised, secure store-of-value chain. Limited smart-contract functionality by design — which is also its strength.

Bitcoin the protocol has the longest unbroken track record in crypto, the largest hash-power security budget, and no central operator. Risk is almost entirely at the wallet/exchange layer, not the chain itself. For long-term storage, hardware-wallet self-custody on Bitcoin is the safest setup we audit.
