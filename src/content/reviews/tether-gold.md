## Tether Gold (XAUT), assessed

**Tether Gold** puts physical gold on-chain: each **XAUT** is backed by **one troy ounce** of allocated gold held in Switzerland, and it's one of the **largest and most liquid** tokenized-gold products around. You can hold it in your own wallet and trade it easily.

The reason it sits just below [PAX Gold](/reviews/pax-gold) for us is **disclosure**: Tether publishes less detail than NYDFS-regulated Paxos does for PAXG. The gold appears to be there; you simply get a bit less to independently verify.

## Verdict

A solid, liquid way to hold gold on-chain — **80/100, green**. If maximum transparency is your priority, PAXG edges it; if liquidity and size matter most, XAUT is excellent. Either way, self-custody it and treat gold as volatile. More options in [tokenized assets](/tokenized-assets).
