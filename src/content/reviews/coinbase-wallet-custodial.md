The custodial side of Coinbase — your crypto sits in Coinbase's wallets, secured by their cold storage and insurance, but you don't hold the keys.

A reasonable on-ramp for absolute beginners who aren't ready for self-custody. Regulated, insured against breaches, and easy to use. But remember: not your keys, not your coins. For long-term holdings, move to a hardware wallet.
