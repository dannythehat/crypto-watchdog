## Crypto.com Visa Card, assessed

The **Crypto.com Visa Card** is a **tiered cashback** card backed by a large, **MiCA-licensed** platform, with **no monthly fee** and broad availability. On paper it's attractive — but the version people remember from **2021 no longer exists**. The headline cashback has been **cut sharply**, and the best rates now require **locking CRO**, the platform's own token, which carries real price risk.

It's also **custodial** (your balance sits with Crypto.com until spent), and — like any crypto card — spending non-stablecoin crypto can be a **taxable event**.

## Verdict

A capable card if you'll actually use it and the **CRO lock-up maths** stacks up for you — **72/100, caution**. If you want simplicity without token strings, the [Coinbase Card](/reviews/coinbase-card) is the cleaner pick. Either way, read the [full Crypto.com review](/reviews/crypto-com) for the whole-platform picture, and compare options in our [crypto cards](/crypto-cards) guide.
