A regulated multi-asset broker offering crypto, stocks, and ETFs alongside its flagship social/copy-trading features. Public company since 2025.

A legitimate, regulated broker — but a poor crypto-native experience. You don't actually own the underlying coins in most jurisdictions; you hold a contract. Fine for casual exposure, wrong for anyone serious about crypto. And copy-trading features encourage the worst possible behaviour.
