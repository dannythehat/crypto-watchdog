A hashpower marketplace where miners rent out compute and buyers pay for mining services. Slovenian company operating since 2014, with a major 2017 hack ($64M) in its history.

The dominant hashpower marketplace, useful for casual miners with consumer GPUs. But the 2017 hack — where 4,700 BTC were stolen from hot wallets — should still inform how much you trust the platform with custody. Withdraw frequently; don't use it as a savings account.
