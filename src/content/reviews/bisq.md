A fully decentralised, peer-to-peer Bitcoin exchange. No KYC, no central servers, no custody — trades happen directly between users via a desktop app.

The most ideologically pure P2P exchange in crypto. If privacy and self-sovereignty matter to you, Bisq is the gold standard. Trade-offs: clunky desktop-only UX, low liquidity, and slow trades. For most users, this is overkill — but for the right user, it's essential.
