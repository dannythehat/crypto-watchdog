## Is Rollbit safe?

Rollbit (since **2020**) is the most **novel** — and most **speculative** — operator in our list. It combines a **casino**, a **sportsbook**, and leveraged **crypto "futures"**, all wrapped around its own **$RLB token** with staking and buybacks. It's popular and fast-moving, but that innovation comes with layered risk, hence a **58/100** provisional score.

## Why it's the riskiest of our non-red picks

Rollbit stacks **three ways to lose at once**: ordinary **gambling** house edge, the **price risk** of holding/earning $RLB, and the **leverage** in its futures products that amplifies losses. For most people that's too much compounded risk. It's not a scam — it's just genuinely high-risk by design.

## Verdict

For advanced, risk-tolerant users only — **58/100, caution** — and even then, avoid the leveraged products and treat $RLB rewards as variable.
