The original play-to-earn phenomenon. Sky Mavis's NFT-based game peaked at $4B in monthly volume in 2021 before its economy collapsed and the Ronin bridge was hacked for $625M.

Historically important but a cautionary tale. The economic model (new players paying old players) was unsustainable, and the Ronin hack exposed bridge risk. Sky Mavis has rebuilt, but treat any "play-to-earn" pitch with deep scepticism.
