## Bybit Copy Trading, assessed

Bybit's **copy-trading desk** is large, slick and popular: detailed lead-trader stats, flexible copy settings, and deep derivatives liquidity behind it. As a piece of software, it's well made.

The risk is the activity, not the app. **You're mirroring leveraged strangers**, performance histories don't predict the future, and your funds sit **custodially** on an exchange with **unresolved regulatory questions** (see the [main Bybit review](/reviews/bybit)). Hence the **caution** rating.

## Verdict

A capable copy desk for experienced, risk-aware users who size small — **66/100, caution**. Check drawdowns, use stop-losses, and compare with [Bitget](/reviews/bitget-copy-trading) and [OKX](/reviews/okx-copy-trading) copy trading.

> Not financial advice. Copy trading can lose money quickly, especially with leverage.
