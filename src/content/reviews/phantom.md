Phantom Wallet is a popular non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet primarily designed for the Solana blockchain, with expanding support for Ethereum and Polygon. Renowned for its intuitive user interface, robust feature set, and strong security architecture, Phantom Wallet provides a foundational tool for users navigating decentralised finance and NFTs across these networks.

Crypto Watchdog assigns Phantom Wallet a Green Alert rating, recognising it as a highly trusted and reliable wallet within the Solana ecosystem. While the wallet itself is broadly credible, users must remain vigilant against the high prevalence of scams, particularly fake tokens and predatory dApps, that often target users on the Solana blockchain. Phantom Wallet provides the tools, but user diligence remains paramount.
