Thomas Crypto Signals (@futuresxsignals) is a 32k-member Telegram channel run by trader "Thomas" (manager @thomasskrypto), publishing crypto futures signals since 2014. Free tier on Telegram, paid VIP tier (~£120/month via Whop). One CryptoWatchdog editor tested the free signals over 3 months and withdrew a small profit — but the channel publishes no independently audited track record, so we rate Orange: legitimate-feeling but unverifiable.

Crypto Watchdog assigns Thomas Crypto Signals an Orange Alert. After three months of editorial testing of the free Telegram signals (small position sizes, manual execution on a regulated exchange), we recorded a modest net profit and a clean withdrawal. That makes Thomas materially better than the average paid signal channel — most of which we caution against in our [Paid Crypto Signal Groups warning](/reviews/crypto-signal-services-general). However, there is still no third-party-verified track record, no published win-rate methodology, and the VIP product is sold off-platform via Whop with limited refund recourse. Treat any subscription as discretionary spending, never bet position sizes you cannot afford to lose, and remember: signal channels do not hold your funds, but they can absolutely cost you them.
