High-throughput Layer 1 with low fees and a large consumer-app ecosystem. Faster and cheaper than Ethereum, with a higher historical incident rate (network outages, memecoin scams) that users should price in.

Solana the chain is fast, cheap and increasingly stable — but it has had multiple multi-hour outages and its dominant use cases (memecoins, leveraged perps) attract a high volume of pump-and-dump activity. Treat the chain as credible; treat the average new Solana token as hostile until proven otherwise.
