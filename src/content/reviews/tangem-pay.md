## Tangem Pay, assessed

Most crypto cards make you hand your funds to the issuer. **Tangem Pay** is the rare exception: built around the maker of the well-regarded **Tangem hardware wallet**, it's designed so you **keep self-custody of your keys** while you tap to pay. For the "not your keys, not your coins" crowd, that's a genuine step forward.

The caveats are simply **newness** — a shorter track record — and **availability**, which is still expanding by region. As with any card, spending non-stablecoin crypto can be a **taxable event**.

## Verdict

A promising, self-custodial card from a trusted name — **78/100, green**. Confirm availability and fees for your country, keep your Tangem backup safe, and spend stablecoins to keep costs down.
