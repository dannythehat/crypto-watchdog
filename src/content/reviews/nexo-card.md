## Nexo Card, assessed

The **Nexo Card** is clever: in credit mode you **spend against your crypto without selling it**, which avoids an immediate taxable disposal. It comes in debit and credit modes, pays crypto cashback, and plugs into the broader Nexo platform.

The reason it's **caution-rated** is the risk you take on for that convenience. Borrowing against volatile collateral means a price drop can trigger **liquidation**; your collateral is **custodial** (you rely on Nexo); and the best **rewards are tied to holding NEXO tokens**. Regulation and availability also vary by region.

## Verdict

Useful for experienced users at **low loan-to-value**, risky for everyone else — **66/100, caution**. Keep leverage low, don't over-buy NEXO to chase tiers, and confirm it's available and regulated where you live.
