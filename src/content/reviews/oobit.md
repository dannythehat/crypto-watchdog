## Oobit, assessed

**Oobit** does one thing and does it cleanly: lets you **tap to pay with crypto** in stores, with **modest fees**. The app is focused and the approach is sensible — exactly what everyday crypto spending should feel like.

What holds the score to **caution** is maturity. It's a **newer player** with a shorter track record than the incumbents, **coverage varies by region**, and — as always — spending non-stablecoin crypto can be a **taxable event**. Verify how funds are held before you load anything substantial.

## Verdict

A reasonable, well-made option for day-to-day crypto spending — **70/100, caution**. Start small, keep only spending money in the app, self-custody the rest, and spend stablecoins to keep costs down.
