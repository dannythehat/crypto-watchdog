While it presents a feature-rich environment for those looking to automate their strategies, our review indicates that users should approach Cryptohopper with a forensic and sceptical mindset due to its subscription-based cost model and ambitious performance claims that require careful scrutiny against actual results.

Cryptohopper receives an Orange Alert rating from Crypto Watchdog. We advise extreme caution.
