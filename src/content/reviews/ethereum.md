The dominant smart-contract Layer 1, securing more value and developer activity than any other chain. Core protocol risk is low; the surrounding ecosystem (apps, bridges, tokens) is where users actually get hurt.

Ethereum the protocol is as credible as crypto gets — multiple client implementations, proof-of-stake with thousands of independent validators, and a multi-year track record without consensus failure. The risk is not "Ethereum" — it is the application layer built on top of it. Verify every contract you sign.
