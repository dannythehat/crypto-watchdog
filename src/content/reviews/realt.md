## RealT, assessed

**RealT** does something genuinely novel: it tokenizes **US rental properties** so you can own a fraction from around **$50** and receive **rent on-chain**. For anyone priced out of property, that's an appealing idea, and the platform provides property-level information up front.

But tokenizing real estate doesn't remove real-estate risk — it adds crypto risk on top. **Liquidity is thin**, so selling can be slow or require a discount; you're exposed to **vacancy, maintenance and falling values**; and you depend on the **issuer/management** structure that holds the property off-chain. Eligibility and tax also vary by jurisdiction.

## Verdict

Interesting and accessible, but firmly **caution-rated at 60/100**: treat RealT as a **long-horizon, illiquid** holding, read each property's disclosures, and never commit money you might need back quickly. Compare with the rest of the [tokenized assets](/tokenized-assets) category.

> Not financial advice. Confirm eligibility and tax treatment before investing.
