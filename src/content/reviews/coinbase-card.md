## Coinbase Card, assessed

The **Coinbase Card** is the low-drama option in crypto cards: a **Visa debit** that spends your Coinbase balance, pays a bit of **crypto cashback**, has **no annual fee**, and is backed by a **regulated, NASDAQ-listed** exchange. You manage it from the Coinbase app, including an instant freeze.

The honest caveats are tax and cost: **spending crypto other than stablecoins can be a taxable event**, and there's a **conversion spread** when you don't spend USDC. Rewards are real but modest next to cards that gate higher cashback behind locked tokens.

## Verdict

One of the most trustworthy crypto cards going — **86/100, green**. Spend USDC to keep things cheap and clean, enable freeze and 2FA, and treat cashback as a bonus.
