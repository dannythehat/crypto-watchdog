## OKX Copy Trading, assessed

OKX bundles **copy trading** into its app, letting you mirror verified spot and futures traders. The advantage over rivals is the venue: OKX is one of the **stronger, more transparent** major exchanges (clear fee display, mature infrastructure), as covered in our [main OKX review](/reviews/okx).

That doesn't remove the core risk. **Copy trading means mirroring leveraged strangers**, past results don't guarantee future ones, and balances are **custodial**. So while it's the best-positioned of the big copy desks, it's still **caution-rated**.

## Verdict

The pick of the major-exchange copy desks for risk-aware users — **70/100, caution**. Choose traders on drawdown not hype, copy small with stops, and compare with [Bitget](/reviews/bitget-copy-trading) and [Bybit](/reviews/bybit-copy-trading) copy trading.

> Not financial advice. Copy trading can lose money quickly, especially with leverage.
