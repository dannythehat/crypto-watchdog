## Bitget Copy Trading, assessed

Bitget built its name on **copy trading**, and the product is genuinely one of the largest and best-tooled in crypto: transparent lead-trader stats, granular controls (copy amount, ratio, stop-loss), and both spot and futures options.

The risk isn't the tooling — it's what you're doing with it. **You're mirroring leveraged strangers**, past performance tells you little about the future, and your funds are **custodial** on a platform whose corporate transparency we rate as mixed (see the [main Bitget review](/reviews/bitget)). That's why this is **caution-rated**.

## Verdict

A powerful copy-trading desk for users who respect leverage and size positions small — **68/100, caution**. Check drawdowns not just returns, copy with stop-losses, and compare with [Bybit](/reviews/bybit-copy-trading) and [OKX](/reviews/okx-copy-trading) copy trading.

> Not financial advice. Copy trading can lose money quickly, especially with leverage.
