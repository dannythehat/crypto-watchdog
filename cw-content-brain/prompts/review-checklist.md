# Human Review Checklist

Use this checklist before moving any content out of draft status.

## Accuracy

- Are all factual claims supported by source notes?
- Are dates, names, domains, and jurisdictions correct?
- Are any claims outdated or dependent on fast-moving events?

## Evidence Quality

- Are primary sources used where possible?
- Are anecdotal reports clearly labeled as unverified?
- Is the confidence level appropriate for the evidence?

## Risk Language

- Is the rating or severity justified by the taxonomy?
- Does the draft avoid unsupported accusations?
- Are user action recommendations proportionate?

## Editorial Fit

- Is the tone calm, useful, and non-sensational?
- Does the content serve CryptoWatchdog readers directly?
- Is the slug clear and durable?

## Publication Gate

Approve only when the reviewer is comfortable with factual accuracy, source quality, and public-facing risk language.
