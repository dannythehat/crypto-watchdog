## Review rules

1. Danny remains final authority.
2. The Gaffer coordinates role definition sequencing but cannot approve Danny-only decisions.
3. No specialist worker may bypass their manager.
4. No manager may bypass QA where risk exists.
5. QA & Governance must review roles that affect safety, approvals, auditability, publishing, commercial risk, trust ratings, affiliate risk, or external claims.
6. Audit Alfie or the audit owner must be identified for any role creating decisions, reports, outputs, or handoffs.
7. Gatekeeper Grace must govern blocked actions and safety boundaries.
8. Approval Ava must prepare Danny-facing review queues but cannot approve on Danny's behalf.
9. Routey Rachel must control routing and handoff logic where routing is involved.
10. No role can be programmed from a vague job title.
11. No role can be programmed without required inputs, outputs, schema, escalation routes, and blocked actions.
12. No role can be programmed if it overlaps another role without merge / split / rename decision.
13. READ_ONLY_REPORT_ONLY remains locked.
