# Watchdog HQ AI Agency Department & Role Blueprint v1

Build: Build 70 — Watchdog HQ AI Agency Department & Role Blueprint v1  
Status: built / ready for review  
Mode: READ_ONLY_REPORT_ONLY  
Owner: Danny  
Executive owner: The Gaffer  
Governance owner: Gatekeeper Grace  
Audit owner: Audit Alfie  

---

## 1. Purpose

Watchdog HQ is a full operating AI agency / AI company / hive-mind workforce command centre for CryptoWatchdog.

It is not a loose content tool, not a generic AI writer, and not a set of disconnected reports.

This document locks the target AI agency department and role blueprint before detailed role programming begins.

The purpose of Build 70 is to define the full company structure department by department, including department heads, managers, specialist workers, validators, QA routes, audit routes, escalation routes, future-only roles, and role-definition gaps that Danny must review before any role is programmed.

Build 70 does not programme any role yet. It defines the company roster so future programming happens in a controlled, agreed, non-random way.

---

## 2. Current Safety Mode

Current system mode remains:

READ_ONLY_REPORT_ONLY

Build 70 does not enable:

- no live publishing
- no live site edits
- no Supabase writes
- no affiliate URL insertion
- no trust-rating edits
- no AI/API calls
- no media generation or download
- no outreach
- no email sending
- no Safe Apply
- no approval for publication
- no deleting or changing production data
- no real content publishing

All outputs remain local, report-only, planning-only, governance-only, and subject to Danny review.

---

## 3. Core Principle

Every Watchdog HQ role must have:

- one department
- one head or manager
- one clear purpose
- one allowed input set
- one allowed output set
- one QA route
- one audit route
- one escalation route
- one blocked-action inheritance
- one maturity status
- one future programming requirement

No role may self-create.

No worker may bypass its department head.

No department head may bypass Gatekeeper Grace where risk exists.

No AI role may approve Danny-only decisions.

No role may be programmed until Danny has reviewed and agreed what the role actually entails.

---

## 4. Role Maturity Statuses

Watchdog HQ roles use the following maturity statuses:

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| registered_only | Role is named or referenced but not yet fully defined. |
| role_card_defined | Role has a basic role card with purpose, inputs, outputs, blocked actions, and routes. |
| contract_defined | Role has a dedicated operating contract or equivalent detailed control document. |
| schema_defined | Role has a defined output schema. |
| validator_defined | Role has a validator or validation route. |
| runnable_later | Role may become a working report-only agent in a later build. |
| future_only_blocked | Role is intentionally blocked until a later approved operating mode. |
