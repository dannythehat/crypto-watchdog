# Keyword Research & SEO Map

Keyword-first content targeting. Until GSC / a keyword tool is connected, volumes
are qualitative (researched intent, not exact numbers); we refine once GSC is live.
Benchmark: well-optimised crypto comparison/guide content converts ~2–4% of
visitors to sign-ups, and a comparison/buying-guide site at 20k–50k monthly
visits can realistically earn meaningful affiliate revenue — so we target
**commercial-intent** and **safety** clusters first.

> funnel into either a warning (no monetisation) or a green review (monetised CTA).

## On-page SEO rules (every article)
- **Primary keyword in:** H1/title, meta title, meta description, first 100 words, one H2, and the slug.
- **Title:** 50–60 chars, keyword near the front. **Meta description:** 140–160 chars with a benefit + the keyword.
- Schema: Article + FAQPage (+ Review for reviews, ItemList for "best of" lists).
- Internal links to/from the relevant review, guide and warning.
- Each new article records its `primary_keyword` in front matter for tracking.

## Workflow
1. Pick a keyword from the clusters above (commercial first).
2. Confirm intent + check the current top results (what's ranking, what's missing).
3. Write keyword-first to the EEAT bar; set title/meta/slug accordingly.
4. After GSC is connected: pull real impressions/positions, double down on
   keywords ranking 5–20 (striking distance) and refresh decaying pages.
